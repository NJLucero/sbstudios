About

Introduction

Located in the Kerrisdale neighbourhood of Vancouver, BC

Storybook Studios is a photography and video production studio that help bring your stories and visions to life. We are a local business and provide individuals a joyful experience that are memorable and fun. Our focus is to get your personality on camera and create a final product that shows the authentic version of yourself.

We offer a wide range of services, from portrait sessions to weddings, family gatherings to corporate events, we have the skills and equipment to capture the essence of any occasion. Our studio is equipped with the latest technology, ensuring that we can create stunning visuals that showcase your story in the best possible way.

Our Goal

We aim in empowering children, families and those young at hearts in telling their stories and capturing it for you to treasure for a lifetime! We believe that everyone has a story to share whether it's their own or something they want to turn to reality, our staff will guide you from any idea you may have.
